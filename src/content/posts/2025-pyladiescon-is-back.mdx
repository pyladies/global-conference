## Save the Date 🗓️
Yes! You read it right—**PyLadiesCon** returns December 5–7, 2025. 100% online conference designed for our global community. 
Talks, workshops, panels, and community fun, all within the comfort of wherever your Wi‑Fi works.

The official PyLadiesCon website is now live! 🎉

- For general information about our conference, visit our new landing page at https://conference.pyladies.com
- Visit the conference website for 2025 at https://2025.conference.pyladies.com

## What to Expect 🌟

PyLadiesCon is more than just live streams—it's a round-the-clock, multilingual Python conference to highlight our underrepresented groups. We know our attendees span the globe, so our program rotates to fit Tokyo mornings, Berlin afternoons, and late-night coding in your secret lair. Whether you fancy catching a keynote in English or a workshop in Spanish, we've got you covered.

And yes, it's completely free! No ticket fees, no fine print—just register and join in. We're dedicated to being inclusive, welcoming beginners, experts, and friends who just want to support the conference. As long as you follow our [Code of Conduct](https://2025.conference.pyladies.com/en/coc/) 📜, you're golden ✨.

---

## Talks, Panels, and Workshops 🎤🗣️🛠️

- **Keynotes**: At least four inspiring talks to kick things off.  
- **Sessions**: 50+ sessions in multiple languages, including real-world case studies.  
- **Panels**: Lively discussions about career growth, diversity, and open spaces chats.  
- **Hands-On Sprints**: Build a joke-bot 🤖 or contribute to open-source, all guided by experienced mentors.

## A Look Back at 2024 📊
Since its launch in 2023, PyLadiesCon has grown rapidly
Last year was a blast:

- **More Registrations:** 732 registered attendees (up from 600 in 2023) 🙌  
- **More Proposals:** Nearly 200 talk proposals (accepted 50) 📈  
- **More Sponsors:** 11 sponsors (up from 8 in 2023) 🥳🥳  
- **$1,520 raised** (more than double our first year!) 🎉🎉

Relive the highlights in our [2024 Recap Blog Post](https://conference.pyladies.com/2024-pyladiescon-ends/) 📝.
