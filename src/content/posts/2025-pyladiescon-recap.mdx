Thanks for joining us at PyLadiesCon!

We are grateful to have you at our conference. With your support, PyLadiesCon is more successful than ever!

For transparency, we have built a [dashboard](https://portal.pyladies.com/stats) where you can review
our conference statistics in real-time. Through the dashboard, you can see the numbers such
registrations info, sponsorships, fundraising progress, as well as volunteer and audience demographics.

Below are some highlights from conference by the time the conference ends.

|                                      | 2023       | 2024       | 2025       |
|--------------------------------------|------------|------------|------------|
| Number of registrations              | 600        | 732        | 906        |
| Number of first time attendee        | -          | -          | 767 (85%)  |
| Number of proposals                  | 164        | 192        | 194        |
| Number of sponsors                   | 8          | 11         | 20         |
| Sponsorships raised                  | $10,500.00 | $10,000.00 | $55,555.00 |
| Number of individual donations       | 58         | 105        | 94         |
| Donations raised                     | $650.00    | $1,520.00  | $2,565.44  |
| Proceeds (sponsorships + donations)  | $11,150.00 | $11,520.00 | $58,120.44 |

We are thrilled to have more registrations, proposals, and donations this year.
We are also grateful for the tremendous support from our sponsors and donors. Together, we raised more than twice
the amount of sponsorships and donations from the last two years combined!

## Donate to PyLadiesCon

Even though our conference is over, you can still make an impact and support us by [donating](https://2025.conference.pyladies.com/en/donate/).


## Nominate an Outstanding PyLady

If you enjoyed the talks, workshops, and activities during the conference, or if any of our volunteers and team members
have been extra helpful to you, we encourage you to show your gratitude by nominating any of them for the [Outstanding
PyLady Award](https://kit.pyladies.com/en/latest/global/award.html). Nominations are accepted year-round, however for
recognition at PyCon US 2026, nominations must be received by December 31, 2025.

Learn about the award recipients from [2024](https://pyladies.com/blog/Inaugural-Winners-of-the-Outstanding-PyLady-Award/outstanding-pylady-winners/)
and [2025](https://pyladies.com/blog/Celebrating-the-Recipients-of-the-Outstanding-PyLady-Awards-2025/outstanding-pylady-winners/).

## Help us improve

We hope you found the event informative, engaging, and valuable for your professional development. To continually
improve our conferences and better serve our community, we invite you to participate in our [post-conference survey](https://forms.gle/GzkQvxxU6dg4gR7v9).

Your feedback is crucial in helping us understand what worked well and where we can enhance future events.
